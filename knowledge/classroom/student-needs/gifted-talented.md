# Gifted & Talented Students

## Characteristics
- Rapid learning with fewer repetitions
- Grasp abstract concepts readily
- Deep focus in areas of interest

## Key Strategies
- **Depth not Volume:** Complexity, novelty, not more work
- **Tiered Assignments:** Different complexity levels for same concept
- **Independent Study:** Pursuit of individual interests
- **Acceleration:** Subject or grade-level when appropriate

## Avoid
- Using gifted students as unpaid tutors
- Simply assigning more work
- Assuming they don't need support
