# Math Intervention Students

## Profile
- Math skills below grade level
- Missing foundational concepts
- May have developed math anxiety

## Key Strategies
- **CRA Approach:** Concrete → Representational → Abstract
- **Explicit Instruction:** Clear modeling of thinking
- **Visual Representations:** Number lines, arrays, models
- **Fact Fluency:** Strategic practice of basics

## Accommodations
- Multiplication charts and reference sheets
- Calculator for concept understanding (not computation)
- Graphic organizers for problem-solving
