# Behavioral Support Students

## Profile
- May have BIPs, 504 plans, or other support plans
- Behavior often reflects missing skills, not willful noncompliance
- Trauma-informed approach essential

## Key Strategies
- **Clear Expectations:** Explicitly teach desired behaviors
- **Predictable Routines:** Structure provides security
- **Positive Reinforcement:** Acknowledge desired behaviors
- **Breaks:** Scheduled movement or calm-down time
- **Choice:** Limited options for sense of control

## De-escalation
- Remain calm, use neutral tone
- Give space, avoid power struggles
- Private conversations rather than public corrections
