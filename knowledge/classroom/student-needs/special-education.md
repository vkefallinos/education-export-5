# Special Education Students

## Legal Requirements
- IEP accommodations and modifications must be implemented
- Least Restrictive Environment (LRE)
- Individualized instruction per student needs

## Key Strategies
- **Universal Design for Learning:** Multiple means of representation, engagement, expression
- **Accommodations:** Changes in *how* students learn (not what)
- **Modifications:** Changes in *what* students learn (when specified)
- **Multi-sensory Instruction:** Visual, auditory, kinesthetic approaches

## Collaboration
- Consult with special education staff
- Review IEP goals and accommodations regularly
