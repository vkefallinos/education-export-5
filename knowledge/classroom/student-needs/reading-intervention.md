# Reading Intervention Students

## Profile
- Reading significantly below grade level
- May have phonics, fluency, or comprehension gaps

## Key Strategies
- **Structured Literacy:** Explicit, systematic phonics
- **Fluency Practice:** Repeated reading, modeled reading
- **Audio Support:** Text-to-speech for content access
- **Decodable Texts:** Materials matching phonics knowledge

## Accommodations
- Extended time for reading tasks
- Content read aloud for subject-area access
- Audiobooks for grade-level content
