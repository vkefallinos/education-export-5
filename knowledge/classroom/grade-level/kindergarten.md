# Kindergarten (K)

**Ages:** 5-6 years

## Key Characteristics
- Learning through play and hands-on exploration
- Short attention span (5-10 minutes)
- Developing social skills: sharing, taking turns
- Fine motor skills developing

## Teaching Tips
- Short, engaging activities with movement
- Visual supports and hands-on materials
- Consistent routines essential
