# Flipped Classroom

## Structure
- **Home:** Video lessons, readings, online explorations
- **Class:** Problem-solving, discussions, projects
- **Teacher Role:** Circulating, conferencing, clarifying

## Advantages
- Students can pause and rewind content
- Self-paced content delivery
- Teacher available when help is needed most

## Considerations
- Technology and internet access needed at home
- Accountability for at-home learning
