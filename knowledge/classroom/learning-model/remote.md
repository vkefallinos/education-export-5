# Remote/Online Learning

## Characteristics
- All instruction occurs online
- Flexible location for students and teacher
- Technology-dependent

## Best For
- Asynchronous content delivery
- Synchronous sessions for community
- Digital collaboration platforms
- Office hours for individual support

## Challenges
- Technology access and support
- Screen time management
- Building community virtually
