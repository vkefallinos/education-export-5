# 1:1 Device Program

## Uses
- Adaptive learning software (IXL, Khan Academy)
- Research and information literacy
- Digital creation (slides, videos, documents)
- Formative assessment tools (Kahoot, Quizizz)
- Collaborative platforms (shared docs)

## Considerations
- Digital citizenship education
- Screen time balance
- Device management and charging
