# Gym/Outdoor Space Access

## Spaces
- Gymnasium: Large indoor space
- Playground/Field: Outdoor active play
- Track/Trail: Walking or running paths

## Uses
- Physical education and motor skills
- Brain breaks and movement
- Outdoor learning and exploration
- Science observations (nature, weather)
- Team building and cooperative games
