# Proficient Level

Solid understanding, independent application.

## Targets
- Apply skills to novel situations
- Explain thinking with clear reasoning
- Make connections across concepts
- Choose appropriate strategies
- Solve multi-step problems

## Support Needs
- Extended challenges and enrichment
- Choice in approach and representation
