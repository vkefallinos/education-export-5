# Advanced Level

Deep understanding, abstract reasoning, synthesis.

## Targets
- Analyze complex problems and situations
- Evaluate arguments and evidence
- Create original solutions or products
- Transfer learning to unfamiliar contexts
- Synthesize information from multiple sources

## Support Needs
- Minimal scaffolding, consultation when needed
- Authentic, real-world challenges
