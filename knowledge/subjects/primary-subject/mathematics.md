# Mathematics

## Core Domains
- **Number & Operations:** Counting, place value, operations, fractions, decimals
- **Algebra:** Patterns, equations, functions, proportional reasoning
- **Geometry & Measurement:** Shapes, attributes, transformations, measurement
- **Statistics & Probability:** Data, graphs, likelihood

## Mathematical Practices
1. Make sense of problems and persevere
2. Reason abstractly and quantitatively
3. Construct arguments and critique reasoning
4. Model with mathematics
5. Use appropriate tools strategically
6. Attend to precision
7. Look for and use structure
8. Express regularity in repeated reasoning
