# Music

## Musical Elements
- **Rhythm:** Beat, meter, tempo, patterns
- **Melody:** Pitch, contour, scales, intervals
- **Harmony:** Chords, accompaniment, texture
- **Form:** AB, ABA, rondo, theme/variation
- **Expression:** Dynamics, timbre, style

## Musical Skills
- Vocal: Posture, breath, pitch matching, part-singing
- Instrumental: Technique, notation, ensembles
- Literacy: Notation, composition, improvisation

## Musical Contexts
Music history, periods, composers, world music, American music
