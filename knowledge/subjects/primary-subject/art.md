# Visual Arts

## Elements of Art
Line, Shape, Form, Color, Texture, Value, Space

## Principles of Design
Balance, Emphasis, Movement, Pattern, Rhythm, Unity, Proportion

## Media & Techniques
- Drawing: Pencil, charcoal, pastel, ink
- Painting: Watercolor, tempera, acrylic
- Sculpture: Clay, found materials
- Printmaking: Relief, stamping
- Collage and mixed media

Periods, styles, artists, cultural contexts, critique
