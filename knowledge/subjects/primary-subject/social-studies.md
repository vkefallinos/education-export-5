# Social Studies

## Core Disciplines
- **History:** Historical thinking, context, evidence, arguments
- **Geography:** Spatial thinking, places, regions, physical/human systems
- **Civics & Government:** Civic life, government structure, constitutional principles, citizenship
- **Economics:** Scarcity, economic systems, markets, national economy, global economy

## Skills
- Critical thinking and analysis
- Primary and secondary source analysis
- Geographic literacy
- Civic participation
