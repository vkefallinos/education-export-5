# Science

## Core Domains
- **Physical Science:** Matter, forces, motion, energy, waves
- **Life Science:** Molecules to organisms, ecosystems, heredity, evolution
- **Earth & Space:** Earth's place, systems, human impact
- **Engineering:** Design process, technology connections

## Science & Engineering Practices
- Asking questions, defining problems
- Developing and using models
- Planning and carrying out investigations
- Analyzing and interpreting data
- Using mathematics and computational thinking
- Constructing explanations, designing solutions
- Engaging in argument from evidence
- Obtaining, evaluating, communicating information
