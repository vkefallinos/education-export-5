# Physical Education

## Motor Skills
- **Locomotor:** Walk, run, hop, gallop, skip, leap, jump
- **Non-Locomotor:** Bend, stretch, twist, balance
- **Manipulative:** Throw/catch, kick/punt, strike, dribble, volley

## Physical Fitness
- **Health-Related:** Cardiovascular, strength, endurance, flexibility, body composition
- **Skill-Related:** Agility, balance, coordination, power, reaction time, speed

## Social Responsibility
Goal setting, cooperation, teamwork, fair play, safety, etiquette
