# Portfolio-Based Assessment

Collection of work gathered over time showing growth and achievement.

## Types
- **Growth Portfolio:** Shows progress and development
- **Showcase Portfolio:** Best work demonstration
- **Process Portfolio:** Documents learning process

## Advantages
- Shows growth over time
- Captures complexity of learning
- Student ownership and agency
