# Formative Assessment

**Assessment FOR Learning** - ongoing checks to guide instruction

## Strategies
- Exit tickets and quick checks
- Think-pair-share and discussion
- Whiteboard responses
- Observation during work
- Questioning and conferences

## Purpose
- Identify who understands and who needs help
- Guide instructional next steps
- Provide feedback to students
