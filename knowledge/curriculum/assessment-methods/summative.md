# Summative Assessment

**Assessment OF Learning** - evaluates learning at the end of instruction

## Types
- End-of-unit tests and projects
- Final exams
- Standardized assessments
- Performance tasks
- Portfolios (cumulative)

## Purpose
- Determine mastery of standards
- Assign grades for reporting
- Evaluate program effectiveness
