# Cambridge International Curriculum

## Pathway
- **Primary:** Ages 5-11
- **Lower Secondary:** Ages 11-14
- **Upper Secondary:** IGCSE, ages 14-16
- **Advanced:** AS & A Levels, ages 16-19

## Key Features
- Internationally recognized qualifications
- Clear progression across stages
- Skills development: critical thinking, problem-solving
- University acceptance worldwide
