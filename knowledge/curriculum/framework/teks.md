# Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS)

- **State Standards:** Texas-specific K-12 curriculum
- **All Subjects:** Comprehensive across all content areas
- **STAAR Alignment:** State assessment aligned to TEKS

**Key Features:**
- Readiness Standards (essential for success)
- Supporting Standards (introduced, not essential)
- Process Standards (ways of thinking within each discipline)
