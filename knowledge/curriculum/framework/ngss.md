# Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS)

## Three Dimensions
1. **Disciplinary Core Ideas:** Physical, Life, Earth/Space Science, Engineering
2. **Science & Engineering Practices:** Questioning, modeling, investigating, analyzing, arguing
3. **Crosscutting Concepts:** Patterns, cause/effect, systems, energy/matter, structure/function

Performance expectations integrate all three dimensions
