# Gamification

Game design elements to increase motivation.

## Best For
- Skill practice and repetition
- Building engagement
- Progress tracking
- Positive class culture

## Key Elements
- Clear goals and rules
- Progress tracking and feedback
- Rewards and recognition
- Narrative or context
- Balanced challenge
