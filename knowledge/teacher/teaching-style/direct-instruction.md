# Direct Instruction

Teacher-led, explicit teaching with clear modeling.

## Best For
- Introducing new skills or content
- Teaching procedures and routines
- Ensuring all students access core content
- Clarifying misconceptions

## Key Elements
- Clear learning objectives
- Modeling and think-alouds
- Guided practice with feedback
- Independent practice
