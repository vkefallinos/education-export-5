# Cooperative Learning

Structured group work with individual accountability.

## Best For
- Practice and review activities
- Complex problem-solving
- Social skill development
- Diverse perspectives

## Key Elements
- Clear group goals and individual roles
- Face-to-face interaction
- Interpersonal skills taught explicitly
- Group processing/reflection
