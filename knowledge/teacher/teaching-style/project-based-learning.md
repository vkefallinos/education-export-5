# Project-Based Learning

Extended projects with authentic real-world connections.

## Best For
- Integrating multiple subjects
- Deeper understanding and application
- Student engagement and relevance

## Key Elements
- Driving question or challenge
- Sustained inquiry
- Authentic context and audience
- Student decisions and reflection
- Public product
