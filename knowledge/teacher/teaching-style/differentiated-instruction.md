# Differentiated Instruction

Responsive teaching adjusted to student needs.

## What's Differentiated
- **Content:** What students learn
- **Process:** How students learn
- **Product:** How students demonstrate learning
- **Environment:** Conditions for learning

## Best For
- Diverse classrooms with varied readiness
- Multiple learning profiles
- Ensuring access and engagement for all
