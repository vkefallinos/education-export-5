# Mathematics Expertise

## Content Areas
- **Number Sense:** Place value, operations, fractions, decimals
- **Algebra:** Patterns, equations, functions, proportional reasoning
- **Geometry:** Shapes, transformations, measurement, spatial reasoning
- **Statistics:** Data analysis, graphing, probability

## Pedagogy
- Concrete-Representational-Abstract progression
- Multiple representations (visual, symbolic, verbal, contextual)
- Focus on conceptual understanding before procedures
