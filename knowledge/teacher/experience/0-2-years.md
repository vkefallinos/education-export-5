# 0-2 Years Experience (Novice)

## Profile
- Building foundations, steep learning curve
- Energy and enthusiasm for new ideas
- Benefits from mentorship and structured guidance

## Strengths
- Open to new approaches
- Current on educational theory
- Technologically adept

## Growth Areas
- Classroom management consistency
- Pacing and planning efficiency
- Parent communication confidence

## Advice
- Focus on a few key practices
- Seek mentorship actively
